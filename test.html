How to Install Our App

For Android Users:

  1. Open the Google Play Store on your Android device.
  2. Search for HonesTurnament in the search bar.
  3. Click on the Install button.
  4. Wait for the app to download and install.
  5. Once installed, click Open to start using the app.
Download on Google Play

For iOS Users:

  1. Open the App Store on your iOS device.
  2. Search for HonesTurnament in the search bar.
  3. Tap on the Get button.
  4. Authenticate the download with your Apple ID or Face ID/Touch ID.
  5. Once installed, tap Open to launch the app.
